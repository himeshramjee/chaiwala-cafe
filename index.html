Welcome to the Chaiwala Cafe!

Chaiwala is produly South African and was started in the Mother City (Cape Town) from a little town called Hout Bay. We're a young Indian family that's using our daily meals to connect and delight our friends, family and neighbours!

What's on the stove?

Our menus are made up of delicious Gujerati snacks and meals - vegetarian only. These are typical in our household and what we've grown up on. In fact, the food we'll serve on any given day is almost always going to be exactly what we'll eat on that day. We see this as giving us a subtle yet important way of staying connected to our customers. We hope that you find delight in every mindful bite of our food.

Menus: Snacks and Hot meals

We've got 2 menus available. The first is a static menu focused on Delicious Snacks. The second is a saak menu (freshly made curry) that will change each week.

Mindful Eating

We're aiming to not be a typcial restaurant or food business. Our focus will always be on sharing our love for each other through food but most importantly in a healthy manner. My husband and I are both mindful of our mental and physical health. We're not where we want to be and the Chaiwala Cafe is a key mechanism of helping us improve ourselves.

This means cooking with some well known healthy alternatives (e.g. coconut oil vs standard vegetable oil), offering many different vegetarian only meals and way more appropriate portion sizes that will fill bellies without, as my hubby always complains, going into a food coma üòÇ.

We hope that our customers will enjoy our homemade meals in a mindful manner and knowing that we're sharing that meal with you in our home. Mindful eating will help us share the different smells, flavours, textures, combos and simple healthy approach we'll offer through our menus.
